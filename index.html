A Place to Stand

I don’t write because I need to be heard — I write because I exist.

This is a space to hold my thoughts up to the light. To see what’s true.
To integrate what I’ve fractured. To grow into someone I can live with.

I’m not here to brand myself or polish a persona.
I’m not here to lecture, perform, or escape the mess.

This isn’t a how-to manual. It’s a signal in the dark,
in case someone like me is out there looking for one.
