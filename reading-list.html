Reading List

"If it’s on here, I’ve devoured it (or I plan to)." These are the books, articles, and podcasts that have caught my eye and fueled my thinking.

Book: Meditations by Marcus Aurelius

A timeless, raw look into the mind of a leader grappling with duty and human nature. It's a foundational text for anyone interested in practical philosophy.

Article: "The Busy Trap" by Tim Kreider

This essay was a wake-up call. It perfectly articulates the difference between being productive and just being busy.

Podcast: Lex Fridman Podcast

The sheer breadth of guests and the depth of conversation are astounding. It's a masterclass in curiosity.
