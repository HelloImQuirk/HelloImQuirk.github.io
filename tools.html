Tools for Introspection

A small workshop of prompts, systems, and guides to help you annotate your own experience.

Journaling Prompts

Use these to get started when the page is blank but the mind is full.

  • What's one "footnote" you'd add to today's main events?
  • Describe a recent feeling using only metaphors of weather or temperature.
  • If your anxiety had a job title, what would it be and why?
  • Write a "user manual" for a specific part of your personality.
